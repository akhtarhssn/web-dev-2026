Introduction: Bangladesh is a riverine country. In addition to the large number of big and small rivers, there are many lakes, bays, and canals, which constitute an extensive network of aquatic communication. So steamers, launches, and boats constantly ply up and down these water-bodies and render a great service to the people. Boats are very popular for domestic transport. A journey by boat is relatively cheap and convenient. It is comfortable and interesting, also. The other day I had an occasion to enjoy a journey by boat. It was a journey from Raita to Selaidah in the district of Kushtia.
